It offers industry-immersive degree programs in fields like Engineering, Pharmacy, and Management, with a focus on skills-integrated, outcome-based learning aligned with the NEP 2020 framework. The university is recognized by UGC, AICTE, and other bodies and has accreditations including an "A" Grade by NAAC.
